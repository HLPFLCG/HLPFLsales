## Assignment of Membership Interest

**THIS AMENDMENT TO OPERATING AGREEMENT** (this "Amendment") is made and entered into as of [Date], by and between:

**ASSIGNOR**: [Your Name], an individual ("Assignor")

**ASSIGNEE**: Zaitsev LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company ("Assignee")

**COMPANY**: Hlpfl Co LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company (the "Company")

---

## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Company was formed pursuant to the Wyoming Limited Liability Company Act;

**WHEREAS**, Assignor is the sole member of the Company, holding 100% of the Membership Interest in the Company;

**WHEREAS**, Assignee desires to acquire all of Assignor's right, title, and interest in the Company, and Assignor desires to sell, assign, and transfer such interest to Assignee; and

**WHEREAS**, the Members desire to amend the Operating Agreement of the Company to reflect such change in ownership.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and obligations contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE I: ASSIGNMENT OF MEMBERSHIP INTEREST

### Section 1.1 Assignment of Interest

Assignor hereby sells, assigns, transfers, and conveys unto Assignee, its successors and assigns, forever, all of Assignor's right, title, and interest in and to the Company, including but not limited to:

(a) 100% of the Membership Interest in the Company;

(b) All rights to receive distributions, allocations, and any other economic benefits from the Company;

(c) All rights to vote on any matter submitted to the Members for a vote;

(d) All rights to manage and operate the Company; and

(e) All other rights, benefits, and privileges of a Member of the Company under the Operating Agreement and applicable law.

### Section 1.2 Consideration

The consideration for this Assignment shall be [Amount] ($[Amount in words]), the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged by Assignor.

### Section 1.3 Effective Time

This Assignment shall be effective as of [Date] (the "Effective Time").

---

## ARTICLE II: AMENDMENT TO OPERATING AGREEMENT

### Section 2.1 Amendment of Membership Interest

The Operating Agreement of the Company is hereby amended to provide that Zaitsev LLC shall be the sole Member of the Company, holding 100% of the Membership Interest.

### Section 2.2 Assignment of Rights and Obligations

Assignee hereby assumes and agrees to perform all obligations of the Member under the Operating Agreement, and shall be entitled to all rights and benefits of the Member thereunder.

### Section 2.3 Successor Member

Assignee shall be deemed to be the "Member" under the Operating Agreement for all purposes, and all references in the Operating Agreement to "Member" shall hereafter refer to Assignee.

---

## ARTICLE III: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

### Section 3.1 Representations of Assignor

Assignor represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignor has full power and authority to enter into this Amendment and to consummate the transactions contemplated hereby;

(b) This Amendment constitutes a valid and binding obligation of Assignor, enforceable in accordance with its terms;

(c) Assignor is the sole and beneficial owner of the Membership Interest being transferred, free and clear of all liens, security interests, and other encumbrances;

(d) There are no claims, actions, suits, or proceedings pending or, to Assignor's knowledge, threatened against Assignor or the Company relating to the Membership Interest or the business of the Company;

(e) Assignor has not received any notice of any violation of any law, ordinance, or regulation relating to the Company's business; and

(f) All tax returns required to be filed by the Company have been filed, and all taxes due and payable by the Company have been paid in full.

### Section 3.2 Representations of Assignee

Assignee represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignee has full power and authority to enter into this Amendment and to consummate the transactions contemplated hereby;

(b) This Amendment constitutes a valid and binding obligation of Assignee, enforceable in accordance with its terms;

(c) Assignee has the financial capacity to fulfill its obligations under the Operating Agreement; and

(d) Assignee is acquiring the Membership Interest for its own account for investment purposes and not with a view to distribution.

---

## ARTICLE IV: COVENANTS

### Section 4.1 Further Assurances

Each party agrees to execute such further documents and take such further actions as may be reasonably requested by the other party to carry out the intent and purposes of this Amendment.

### Section 4.2 Cooperation

The parties agree to cooperate fully with each other in connection with the transfer of the Membership Interest and the amendment of the Operating Agreement, including without limitation the execution and delivery of any additional documents or instruments reasonably required to effectuate the transactions contemplated hereby.

### Section 4.3 Notice to Third Parties

The parties agree to promptly notify all third parties with whom the Company has material contracts or relationships of the change in ownership of the Company, and to take such actions as may be necessary or appropriate to assign such contracts and relationships to Assignee.

---

## ARTICLE V: INDEMNIFICATION

### Section 5.1 Indemnification by Assignor

Assignor agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Assignee and the Company from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, claims, demands, actions, suits, or proceedings (collectively, "Losses") arising out of or relating to any breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant made by Assignor in this Amendment, or any violation of law occurring prior to the Effective Time.

### Section 5.2 Indemnification by Assignee

Assignee agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Assignor from and against any and all Losses arising out of or relating to any breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant made by Assignee in this Amendment, or any violation of law occurring on or after the Effective Time.

---

## ARTICLE VI: GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### Section 6.1 Governing Law

This Amendment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Wyoming, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

### Section 6.2 Dispute Resolution

Any dispute, controversy, or claim arising out of or relating to this Amendment, including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination, shall be resolved by binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration shall be conducted in Cheyenne, Wyoming, and the arbitrator's award shall be final and binding.

---

## ARTICLE VII: MISCELLANEOUS

### Section 7.1 Entire Agreement

This Amendment constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof, and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations, whether written or oral.

### Section 7.2 Amendments and Waivers

This Amendment may not be amended or modified except by a written instrument signed by all parties. No waiver of any provision of this Amendment shall be deemed a waiver of any other provision or of the same provision on any other occasion.

### Section 7.3 Severability

If any provision of this Amendment is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect, and the invalid, illegal, or unenforceable provision shall be deemed replaced by a valid provision that as closely as possible approximates the intent and economic effect of the invalid provision.

### Section 7.4 Counterparts

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument. Facsimile and electronic signatures shall be deemed original signatures for all purposes.

### Section 7.5 Binding Effect

This Amendment shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties hereto and their respective successors and assigns.
